Machine Learning Enhanced Trading Strategy on Tencent Daily Stock Data (Index Enhancement)

Zepeng Lin, Guanyu Xin, Yuxuan Xue
South China University of Technology (SCUT)
Instructors: Jin Xu, Qi Liu
Report (PDF) Code Data Results

We build an end-to-end pipeline including data preprocessing, factor mining, machine learning forecasting, and an enhanced trading strategy with risk control. The goal is to achieve excess return over a baseline strategy while keeping maximum drawdown as low as possible.

Training: 2018–2023  |  Backtest: 2024-01-01 to 2025-04-24  |  Initial Capital: 100,000 CNY

Assignment Requirements

  1. Data preprocessing: clean the provided Tencent daily stock data and mine informative factors.
  2. Machine learning forecasting: build a model with good predictive performance for stock prices/returns.
  3. Trading strategy: generate buy/sell timing signals from predictions to achieve index enhancement (excess return).
  4. Risk control: maximize profit while keeping maximum drawdown as low as possible.
  5. Visualization & evaluation: visualize final investment results and evaluate against actual data.
  6. Report: clearly describe algorithms, training procedure, feature engineering, preprocessing, and advantages of ML for this task.

Raw Stock Data

We use daily OHLCV-style data for Tencent stock. Typical fields include Open, High, Low, Close, Volume (and potentially additional fields depending on the dataset). We split the timeline into:

  • Training set: 2018-01-01 to 2023-12-31 (for feature/parameter learning)
  • Backtest set: 2024-01-01 to 2025-04-24 (for out-of-sample strategy evaluation)

Factor Mining Results

We mine predictive factors from price and volume information. Categories include:

  • Momentum: past returns over multiple horizons, trend indicators.
  • Volatility: rolling standard deviation, ATR-like measures, intraday range proxies.
  • Technical indicators: moving averages, RSI, MACD, Bollinger-related features.
  • Volume/flow: volume change rates, turnover proxies, price-volume divergence signals.

We evaluate factors via correlation with future returns, information coefficient (IC), stability across time, and feature importance from the trained model.

Strategy Design and Multi-Strategy Comparison

We convert model predictions into trading signals and compare multiple strategies. Example candidates:

  • Buy & Hold: baseline holding Tencent stock throughout the backtest window.
  • MA Crossover: a purely technical baseline strategy.
  • Prediction Threshold Strategy: go long when predicted return exceeds a threshold, otherwise stay in cash.
  • Risk-Controlled Strategy: apply position sizing and drawdown constraints to reduce tail risk.

For each strategy, we evaluate net value curve, excess return over baseline, drawdown, turnover, and risk-adjusted metrics.
